# Economic Capabilities — User Level Spec

**Type:** Specification | **Date:** 2026-04-04 | **MS§:** §5.14 | **Service:** `services/commerce/`

---

## Capability Domain: §5.14 Economic Capabilities (User Level)

Covers: revenue participation | earnings tracking | payout calculation

---

## Scope

User-level economic capabilities define how individual platform participants (instructors, tutors, academy owners, content creators) participate in platform revenue. This is distinct from platform-level system economics (§5.15) and student-facing financial operations (§5.3).

---

## Capabilities Defined

### CAP-REVENUE-PARTICIPATION
- Defines how a platform participant earns from the revenue generated by their content, sessions, or students
- Participation types: percentage of enrollment revenue, per-session fee, fixed monthly allocation
- Owner: `services/commerce/owner_economics.py`
- Shared model: `shared/models/owner_economics.py`, `shared/models/teacher_economics.py`

### CAP-EARNINGS-TRACKING
- Real-time tracking of a participant's earned revenue across all earning sources
- Inputs: payment events, enrollment completions, session completions
- Outputs: earnings ledger per participant, period-wise breakdown
- Owner: `services/commerce/owner_economics.py`
- Test coverage: `services/commerce/test_owner_economics.py`

### CAP-PAYOUT-CALCULATION
- Calculates gross earnings, deductions (platform fee, tax, adjustments), and net payout per participant per period
- Payout schedule: configurable (weekly, monthly, on-demand) via config service
- Owner: `services/commerce/owner_economics.py`
- Validation: `validation/tests/test_exam_economics_validation.py`

---

## Boundary Rules

- User economics is a sub-domain of commerce — it does NOT own invoice or billing SoR
- Teacher economics (`shared/models/teacher_economics.py`) and owner economics share the same capability but serve different participant types
- Payout execution (bank transfer, wallet credit) routes through the payment adapter — not owned by this service
